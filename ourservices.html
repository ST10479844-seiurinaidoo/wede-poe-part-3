Our Specialties

Here's a glimpse of what you can expect at Green Pulse Solar Solutions:

Maintenance

Regular maintenance is key to ensuring your solar system performs efficiently over time. Our team conducts scheduled inspections, cleans panels, checks wiring and inverters, and resolves any technical issues promptly.

Installation

Our professional installation services ensure that your solar system is safe, efficient, and tailored to your specific needs. We assess your property, design an optimal system layout, and install high-quality solar panels and inverters with minimal disruption to your daily activities.

Consultation

Our consultation service guides you through every step of going solar. We perform detailed energy assessments, evaluate your electricity usage, and recommend the best solar system to meet your energy goals.

Monitoring

We provide real-time monitoring tools so you can track your system's performance online or via an app. Our support team is always available to assist with troubleshooting, warranty claims, and advice on optimizing energy output.

Battery Storage

Maximize your solar energy usage with our advanced battery storage solutions. Store excess energy for nighttime or load-shedding periods and enjoy uninterrupted power supply.

Custom Solutions

Every client has unique energy requirements. We offer customized packages for residential, commercial, and institutional clients that combine solar panels, inverters, monitoring systems, and battery storage. Our experts design systems that maximize savings, reliability, and sustainability.

Sustainabillity

Choosing Green Pulse Solar Solutions helps reduce your carbon footprint and supports South Africa's transition to clean energy. By installing solar systems, clients contribute to lowering greenhouse gas emissions and promoting environmental awareness in their communities.
