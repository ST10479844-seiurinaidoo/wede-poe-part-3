## Overview:
Green Pulse Solar Solutions is a modern, responsive website designed to promote clean and renewable solar energy solutions. The site provides users with information about the company, its services, and its contact details while promoting environmental awareness through sustainable energy initiatives.

## Pages Summary:
### Home Page (homepage.html)
1. Introduction Green Pulse Solar Solutions.
2. Includes a dynamic background video header.
3. Features navigation links to other sections of the site.
4. Promotes clean energy and sustainability awareness.

### Contact Page (contactus.html)
1. Contains a contact form for user inquiries.
2. Displays contact information such as phone number, email, and location.

### About Page (aboutus.html)
1. Provides company background, mission, and core values.
2. Highlights Green Pulse’s contribution to environmental sustainability.

### Services Page (ourservices.html)
1. Lists all available services such as installation, maintenance, consultation, and monitoring.
2. Includes a search feature to help users quickly find specific services.

### FAQ'S Page (frequentlyaskedquestions.html)
1. Accordion feature for frequently asked questions.
2. Youtube videos answering any further questions.
